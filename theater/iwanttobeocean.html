i want to obe ocean (2024)

‘I Want To Be Ocean’ presents life cycles that embed and overlap each other in order to complement each other from the indeterminate past into a certain future. It works on the identifications of the imagined other, the desiring and adoring, but also the absent, which is reinterpreted through its own power.

‘we three
we three
i bend for the sake of the existence of us
we three

in what kind of common we does the I remain - can the I still remain? must perhaps even the I remain so that the we becomes all the more possible?

I I I
accept my I, me
so that I, you, your you, your ‘I’, can also accept it
create space together for the one yours, your you, mine too ...’

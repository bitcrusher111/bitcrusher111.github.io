<div class="text-container">
  <p>
  CRTTR is a music label focusing on the release of experimental club music. Founded as a platform for innovative electronic sounds, CRTTR bridges the gap between underground club culture and experimental music production.
  <br><br>
  The label has hosted numerous showcase events and live performances across Switzerland, including regular appearances at venues like Kapitel Bern, TRNSTN, and Radio Bollwerk. Through its releases and events, CRTTR creates space for artists who push the boundaries of electronic music.
  <br><br>
  </p>
